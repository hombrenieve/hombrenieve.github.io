# 📚 Cambridge B1 Preliminary (PET) Grammar Cheatsheet

## 1. Present Simple vs. Present Continuous

- **Present Simple:** Habits, routines, and facts.
    
    - _Example:_ I go to school every day.
        
- **Present Continuous:** Actions happening **now**.
    
    - _Example:_ She is studying English now.
        

## 2. Past Simple

- **Usage:** Completed actions in the past.
    
    - _Example:_ I visited Colombo last week. / He watched a movie yesterday.
        

## 3. Past Continuous

- **Usage:** Action in progress at a past time or interrupted by another action.
    
    - _Example:_ I was reading when she called.
        

## 4. Present Perfect

- **Usage:** Life experiences or actions with results in the present.
    
    - _Example:_ I have never been to Italy. / She has already finished her homework.
        

## 5. Will vs. Going To

- **Will:** Instant decisions or predictions. (_I will help you._)
    
- **Going to:** Planned actions or intentions. (_I am going to visit my uncle._)
    

## 6. Comparatives & Superlatives

- **Comparative:** Comparing two things. (_This book is **better than** that one._)
    
- **Superlative:** Comparing three or more. (_She is **the tallest** in the class._)
    

## 7. Modals

- **Can:** Ability (_He can swim_)
    
- **Should:** Advice (_You should study_)
    
- **Must:** Obligation (_You must go_)
    
- **Might:** Possibility (_It might rain_)
    

## 8. Conditionals

- **Zero:** Facts. (_If you heat water, it boils._)
    
- **First:** Real future possibilities. (_If it rains, I will stay home._)
    
- **Second:** Imaginary/Unlikely situations. (_If I had more money, I would travel._)
    

## 9. Question Forms

- **Wh- Questions:** _Where do you live?_
    
- **Tag Questions:** _You're coming, aren't you?_
    

## 10. Nouns & Quantifiers

- **Countable/Uncountable:** _Some, any, much, many, a lot of, few, little._
    
    - _Example:_ I have a **few** friends. / Is there **any** milk?
        

---

## 11. Articles

- **a / an:** General things. (_I saw **a** dog._)
    
- **the:** Specific things already mentioned. (_The dog barked at me._)
    

## 12. Pronouns

- **Subject/Object:** _She_ likes _him_.
    
- **Possessive:** That pen is _mine_.
    
- **Reflexive:** He cut _himself_.
    

## 13. Adverbs of Frequency

- _Always, usually, often, sometimes, never._
    
    - _Example:_ I **always** brush my teeth.
        

## 14. Prepositions of Time & Place

- **Place:** _The book is **on** the table._
    
- **Time:** _I was born **in** June._ (In/On/At/Under/Next to)
    

## 15. Gerunds & Infinitives

- **Gerund (-ing):** _I enjoy **reading**._
    
- **Infinitive (to + verb):** _I want **to go**._
    

## 16. Passive Voice

- **Usage:** The object becomes the subject.
    
    - **Present:** _English is spoken worldwide._
        
    - **Past:** _The cake was baked by my mother._
        

## 17. Reported Speech

- **Statements:** _He said he **was** tired._ (Tense shifts back).
    
- **Questions:** _She asked if I **liked** coffee._
    

## 18. Relative Clauses

- Use **who** (people), **which** (things), or **that** (both).
    
    - _Example:_ The man **who** lives next door is a doctor.
